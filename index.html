SIT223/SIT753 - Professional Practice in Information Technology

This unit introduces students to the theory and practice of modern software development and operations. Topics include agile methodologies, continuous integration and deployment, DevOps culture, version control, cloud infrastructure, and automated testing strategies to deliver high-quality software efficiently. Students will gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and practices used in real-world software engineering teams.
